Chilli Con Carne

This is a recipe I rely on for lunch every day. With plenty flavour and nutrition it needs to be in everyone's recipe book.

It is a spicy stew containing chili peppers (sometimes in the form of chili powder), meat (usually beef), tomatoes, and often pinto beans or kidney beans.

Ingredients

  • 2kg Minced Beef
  • 4 x Packets of Chilli Con Carne Spices
  • 2 x Cans of Kidney Beans
  • 2 x Cans of Black Beans
  • 2 x Diced Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook minced beef until soft
  2. Pour in 2 packets of chilli con carne spices
  3. Drain and rinse kidney beans and black beans
  4. Add to pot
  5. Add diced tomatoes
  6. Mix well then bring to simmer
  7. Let simmer on low for 45 minutes
